# Privacy Policy  
**Last Updated:** November 8, 2025  

This Privacy Policy explains how **Active Hunter** (“the Bot”) collects, uses, and protects information while you interact with it on Discord.  

---

## 1. Information We Collect
The Bot may collect:
- **Discord IDs:** User, server, or channel IDs to identify and respond to commands.  
- **Command Data:** Information related to user interactions (e.g., commands used and responses generated).  
- **Temporary Logs:** Error logs for debugging and maintenance.  

The Bot does **not** collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or payment details.  

---

## 2. How We Use Information
Collected data is used only to:
- Operate and maintain the Bot’s functionality.  
- Provide responses to user commands.  
- Improve performance or fix issues.  

No collected data is shared with third parties.  

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security
- Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for the Bot’s operation.  
- We do not sell or share data with external parties.  
- Users may request deletion of their data by contacting the developer.  

---

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Bot runs on Discord’s platform and uses its API to function.  
Please refer to [Discord’s Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) for more information on how Discord handles user data.  

---

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Continued use of the Bot after updates means you accept the revised policy.
